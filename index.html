I grow approximately 1000 named daylilies and a few thousand of my own seedlings. My collection includes a wide variety of forms, including spiders, unusual forms, and doubles. My current hybridizing focus is double and white daylilies. I have been an AHS Display Garden for several years.

Please email me to check availability at Kaymcline@gmail.com. Let me know when you want your plants shipped. Sometimes, I may be able to sell a double fan of items that are “display (list will not show price)”. I accept check or Paypal payment.

SHIPPING INSTRUCTIONS

I will ship Priority Mail unless you indicate a preference for UPS. Please be sure to give us your UPS address. I ship the same day I dig, Monday or Tuesday, to ensure delivery before the weekend. Shipping charges are $10.00 plus 1.00 for each plant for Priority Mail and $15.00 plus 1.00 for each plant UPS Ground. Your first plant is included in the base shipping regardless of shipping method.

Note that rust can overwinter in our mild southern climate and we cannot gaurantee rust-free plants.
