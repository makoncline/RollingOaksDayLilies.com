Daylily description

The daylily Double Sunburst was hybridized by Guidry in 1987. The color of Double Sunburst can be described as cream blend with green throat. It is a Diploid. It has a Double form. It blooms in (Early-Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (6 in, (15 cm)). It has a normal scape height of (23 in, (58 cm)). It is Fragrant.
