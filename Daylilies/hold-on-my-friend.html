Daylily description

The daylily Hold On My Friend was hybridized by Carpenter-J. in 2009. Hold On My Friend was hybridized by crossing (Radiant Magic x Lonnie Leroy Carpenter). The color of Hold On My Friend can be described as hot coral orange with deeper eye above gold throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 22. It blooms in (Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (7 in, (18 cm)). It has 5 branches. It has a normal scape height of (24 in, (61 cm)). It is Fragrant.
