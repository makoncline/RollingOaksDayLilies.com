Suburban Coach G.

The daylily Suburban Coach G. was hybridized by Watts in 2015. Suburban Coach G. was hybridized by crossing (sdlg x Clark Gable). The color of Suburban Coach G. can be described as rose pink with light purple halo and green to yellow throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a Double 100% form. It has a bud count of 12. It blooms in (Early-Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (5.25 in, (13 cm)). It has 3 branches. It has a normal scape height of (21 in, (53 cm)).
