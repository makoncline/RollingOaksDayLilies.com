Daylily description

The daylily Peggy Jeffcoat was hybridized by Joiner-J. in 1995. Peggy Jeffcoat was hybridized by crossing (Jean Swann × Rebecca Marie). The color of Peggy Jeffcoat can be described as yellow self with light green throat. It is a Diploid. It has a Double form. It blooms in (Midseason-Late,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (6.5 in, (17 cm)). It has a normal scape height of (18 in, (46 cm)).
