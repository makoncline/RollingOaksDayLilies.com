Lemon Curls

The daylily Lemon Curls was hybridized by Waldrop-D. in 2009. Lemon Curls was hybridized by crossing (sdlg x Heavenly Angel Ice). The color of Lemon Curls can be described as lemon ivory blend above green throat. It is a Diploid. It has a Unusual FormCrispate form. It has a bud count of 38. It blooms in (Early). It's flowers measure (5.5 in, (14 cm)). It has 5 branches. It has a normal scape height of (42 in, (107 cm)).
