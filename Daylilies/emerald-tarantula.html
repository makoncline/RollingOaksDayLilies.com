Daylily description

The daylily Emerald Tarantula was hybridized by Pierce-G. in 2015. Emerald Tarantula was hybridized by crossing (Rolling Raven x Tet. Rose F. Kennedy). The color of Emerald Tarantula can be described as large red with wax like substance above a large green throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a Unusual FormCascade form. It has a bud count of 35. It blooms in (Early-Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (11.5 in, (29 cm)). It has 5 branches. It has a normal scape height of (36 in, (91 cm)).
