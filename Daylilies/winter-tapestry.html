Daylily description

The daylily Winter Tapestry was hybridized by Salter-E.H. in 1996. The color of Winter Tapestry can be described as cream white with blue violet washed eyezone and green throat. It is a Tetraploid. It blooms in (Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (3 in, (8 cm)). It has a normal scape height of (18 in, (46 cm)).
