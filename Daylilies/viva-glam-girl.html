Viva Glam Girl


Share this daylily on Facebook


Daylily description

The daylily Viva Glam Girl was hybridized by Marchant in 2011. Viva Glam Girl was hybridized by crossing (Heavenly Fission x Barry Goldwater). The color of Viva Glam Girl can be described as salmon cream pink with pink eye and partial picotee, edge can show small serrated teeth, takes many forms of double from crested to peony. It is a Tetraploid. It has a Unusual FormCrispateDouble 95% form. It has a bud count of 25. It blooms in (Early-Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (7.25 in, (18 cm)). It has 5 branches. It has a normal scape height of (31 in, (79 cm)). It is Fragrant.
