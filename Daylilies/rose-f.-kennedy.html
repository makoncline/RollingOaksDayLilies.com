Daylily description

The daylily Rose F. Kennedy was hybridized by Doorakian in 2007. Rose F. Kennedy was hybridized by crossing (Emerald Starburst x sdlg). The color of Rose F. Kennedy can be described as green with wide purple red picotee edge above green throat. It is a Diploid. It has a bud count of 24. It blooms in (Midseason). It's flowers measure (7.5 in, (19 cm)). It has 4 branches. It has a normal scape height of (29 in, (74 cm)).
