Daylily description

The daylily Double Explosion was hybridized by Gossard-D. in 2008. Double Explosion was hybridized by crossing ((John Kirkland × Cardigan Bay) × Tet. Double Pompon). The color of Double Explosion can be described as salmon pink with wine red eye pink yellow wire edge. It is a Tetraploid. It has a Double 99% form. It has a bud count of 24. It blooms in (Midseason). It's flowers measure (6.5 in, (17 cm)). It has 4 branches. It has a normal scape height of (36 in, (91 cm)). It is Fragrant.
