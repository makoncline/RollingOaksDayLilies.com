Daylily description

The daylily Gentle Shepherd was hybridized by Yancey in 1980. Gentle Shepherd was hybridized by crossing (sdlg x sdlg). The color of Gentle Shepherd can be described as near white self with yellow green throat. It is a Diploid. It blooms in (Early-Midseason). It's flowers measure (5 in, (13 cm)). It has a normal scape height of (29 in, (74 cm)).
