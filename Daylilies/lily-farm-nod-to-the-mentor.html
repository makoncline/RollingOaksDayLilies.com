Daylily description

The daylily Lily Farm Nod to the Mentor was hybridized by Carpenter-M. in 2017. Lily Farm Nod to the Mentor was hybridized by crossing (sdlg × Red on Red). The color of Lily Farm Nod to the Mentor can be described as Crimson pink blend with a golden yellow edge and heavily ruffled with a green yellow throat.. It is a Tetraploid. It has a Double 80% form. It has a bud count of 15. It blooms in (Late,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (6.5 in, (16 cm)). It has 3 branches. It has a normal scape height of (24 in, (60 cm)). It is Fragrant.
