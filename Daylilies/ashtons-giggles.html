Ashton's Giggles


Share this daylily on Facebook


Daylily description

The daylily Ashton's Giggles was hybridized by Phelps in 2006. Ashton's Giggles was hybridized by crossing ((Destined to See × sdlg) × Awesome Blossom). The color of Ashton's Giggles can be described as cream with huge violet eye and violet silver braided edge above green extending to yellow throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 20. It blooms in (Early,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (6 in, (15 cm)). It has 3 branches. It has a normal scape height of (26 in, (66 cm)).
