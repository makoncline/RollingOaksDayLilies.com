Daylily description

The daylily Radiant Simplicity was hybridized by Begnaud in 2000. Radiant Simplicity was hybridized by crossing (Pure and Simple x Yuma). The color of Radiant Simplicity can be described as orange with dark red eye and wire red edge above green throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 24. It blooms in (Early,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (5.5 in, (14 cm)). It has 4 branches. It has a normal scape height of (28 in, (71 cm)). It is Fragrant.
