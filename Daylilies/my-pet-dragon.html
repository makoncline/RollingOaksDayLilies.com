Daylily description

The daylily My Pet Dragon was hybridized by Selman in 2019. My Pet Dragon was hybridized by crossing (Halloween Dragon × Wacky Dragon). The color of My Pet Dragon can be described as Pumpkin orange with red eye and matching picotee over large yellow to green throat.. It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 18. It blooms in (Midseason). It's flowers measure (6.5 in, (16 cm) ). It has 3 branches. It has a normal scape height of (34 in, (86 cm) ).
