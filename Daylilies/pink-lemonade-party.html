Daylily description

The daylily Pink Lemonade Party was hybridized by Salter in 2006. The color of Pink Lemonade Party can be described as pink medium pink bitone with cream edge above green throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 30. It blooms in (Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (5.5 in, (14 cm)). It has 4 branches. It has a normal scape height of (28 in, (71 cm)). It is Fragrant.
