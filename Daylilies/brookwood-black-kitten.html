Daylily description

The daylily Brookwood Black Kitten was hybridized by Sharp in 1995. The color of Brookwood Black Kitten can be described as black red self with green throat. It is a Diploid. It blooms in (Midseason). It's flowers measure (2.75 in, (7 cm)). It has a normal scape height of (22 in, (56 cm)). It is Fragrant.
