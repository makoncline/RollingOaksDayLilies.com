Jelly filled donut

The daylily Jelly filled donut was hybridized by Eller-N. in 2004. Jelly filled donut was hybridized by crossing (Double Dipper x Alexander's Ragtime Band). The color of Jelly filled donut can be described as bright yellow with red eye above green throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a Double 96% form. It blooms in (Early,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (5 in, (13 cm)). It has a normal scape height of (24 in, (61 cm)). It is Fragrant.
