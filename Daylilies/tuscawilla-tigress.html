Daylily description

The daylily Tuscawilla Tigress was hybridized by Hansen in 1988. Tuscawilla Tigress was hybridized by crossing (Seductor x Tet. Pat Mercer). The color of Tuscawilla Tigress can be described as bright orange with dark orange eyezone and chartreuse throat. It is a Tetraploid. It blooms in (Early-Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (7.25 in, (18 cm)). It has a normal scape height of (25 in, (64 cm)).
