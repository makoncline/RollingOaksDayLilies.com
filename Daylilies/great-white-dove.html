Great White Dove


Share this daylily on Facebook


Daylily description

The daylily Great White Dove was hybridized by Pierce-G. in 2012. Great White Dove was hybridized by crossing (Shamrock Dew × Fancy Lace). The color of Great White Dove can be described as white self, ruffled edges with angel wings and green throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 50. It blooms in (Extra Early,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (6.5 in, (17 cm)). It has 9 branches. It has a normal scape height of (38 in, (97 cm)). It is Fragrant.
