Spacecoast Two Eyed Jack

Daylily description

The daylily Spacecoast Two Eyed Jack was hybridized by Kinnebrew-J. in 2006. Spacecoast Two Eyed Jack was hybridized by crossing (Gemini Jack x sdlg). The color of Spacecoast Two Eyed Jack can be described as cream with purple eye and picotee. It is a Tetraploid. It has a Double 90% form. It has a bud count of 16. It blooms in (Early-Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (6 in, (15 cm)). It has 2 branches. It has a normal scape height of (24 in, (61 cm)).
