Daylily description

The daylily The King Is Coming was hybridized by Carpenter-J. in 2004. The King Is Coming was hybridized by crossing (Evening Enchantment x Mrs John Cooper). The color of The King Is Coming can be described as royal purple with silvery lavender gray watermark and edge above green throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 26. It blooms in (Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (5.5 in, (14 cm)). It has 5 branches. It has a normal scape height of (26 in, (66 cm)). It is Fragrant.
