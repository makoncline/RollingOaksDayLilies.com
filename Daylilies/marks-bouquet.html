Daylily description

The daylily Mark's Bouquet was hybridized by Agosta in 2006. Mark's Bouquet was hybridized by crossing (Vincenza × Nebuchadnezzar's Furnace). The color of Mark's Bouquet can be described as yellow overlayed with red dots and eye above small green throat. It is a Diploid. It has a bud count of 22. It blooms in (Early,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (4.25 in, (11 cm)). It has 4 branches. It has a normal scape height of (22 in, (56 cm)).
