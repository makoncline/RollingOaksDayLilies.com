Daylily description

The daylily Indian Sky was hybridized by Farris in 1963. The color of Indian Sky can be described as deep rich gold with dark purple eyezone and green throat. It is a Diploid. It blooms in (Midseason). It's flowers measure (5.5 in, (14 cm)). It has a normal scape height of (22 in, (56 cm)).
