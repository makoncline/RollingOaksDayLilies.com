Daylily description

The daylily Legro Indian Chief was hybridized by Grovenstein-L.&E. in 2010. Legro Indian Chief was hybridized by crossing (Fire and Wind x (Blazing Autumn Sun x (Highland Lord x Champagne Chilling))). The color of Legro Indian Chief can be described as red with lighter red midrib and edge slightly darker eye above a yellow throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a Double 99% form. It has a bud count of 15. It blooms in (Midseason-Late). It's flowers measure (5.75 in, (15 cm)). It has 3 branches. It has a normal scape height of (29 in, (74 cm)).
