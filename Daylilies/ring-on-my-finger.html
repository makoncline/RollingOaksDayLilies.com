Daylily description

The daylily Ring On My Finger was hybridized by Grovenstein-L.&E. in 2004. Ring On My Finger was hybridized by crossing (((sdlg x Lil's Pet) x (Coyote Moon x Angel's Smile)) x Key to My Heart). The color of Ring On My Finger can be described as pink with rose eye and edge above yellow to green throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 27. It blooms in (Midseason). It's flowers measure (4.5 in, (11 cm)). It has 5 branches. It has a normal scape height of (22 in, (56 cm)).
