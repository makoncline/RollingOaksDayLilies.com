Daylily description

The daylily Making An Impact was hybridized by Joiner-J. in 2011. Making An Impact was hybridized by crossing (sdlg × sdlg). The color of Making An Impact can be described as burgundy self above a yellow to green throat. It is a Diploid. It has a Double 95% form. It has a bud count of 15. It blooms in (Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (5.5 in, (14 cm) ). It has 2 branches. It has a normal scape height of (34 in, (86 cm) ). It is Fragrant.
