Daylily description

The daylily Spacecoast Dark Lord was hybridized by Kinnebrew-J. in 2009. Spacecoast Dark Lord was hybridized by crossing (Karen Stephens × Spacecoast Dark Obsession). The color of Spacecoast Dark Lord can be described as black red with gold edge. It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 22. It blooms in (Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (6 in, (15 cm)). It has 3 branches. It has a normal scape height of (28 in, (71 cm)).
