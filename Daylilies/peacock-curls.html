Peacock Curls

The daylily Peacock Curls was hybridized by Sheppard-Faggard in 1995. Peacock Curls was hybridized by crossing (Peacock Alley x sdlg). The color of Peacock Curls can be described as cream with rose-lavender eyezone above lime green throat. It is a Diploid. It blooms in (Midseason-Late). It's flowers measure (5.5 in, (14 cm)). It has a normal scape height of (20 in, (51 cm)).
