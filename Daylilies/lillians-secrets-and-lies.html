Daylily description

The daylily Lillian's Secrets and Lies was hybridized by Manning in 2016. Lillian's Secrets and Lies was hybridized by crossing (Tet. Lillian's Thin Ice × Dragonfly Dawn). The color of Lillian's Secrets and Lies can be described as cream white over a green throat, fertile both ways. It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 18. It blooms in (Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (6 in, (15 cm)). It has 3 branches. It has a normal scape height of (24 in, (61 cm)).
