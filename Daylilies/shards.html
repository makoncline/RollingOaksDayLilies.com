Daylily description

The daylily Shards was hybridized by Lambertson in 2015. Shards was hybridized by crossing (Dream Window × sdlg.). The color of Shards can be described as light rose purple, lighter sepals, blue purple eye and edge, white cream teeth, green throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 18. It blooms in (Early,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (6 in, (15 cm) ). It has 3 branches. It has a normal scape height of (30 in, (76 cm) ).
