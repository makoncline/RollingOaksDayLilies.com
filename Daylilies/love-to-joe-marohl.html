Daylily description

The daylily Love to Joe Marohl was hybridized by Elliott-D. in 2017. Love to Joe Marohl was hybridized by crossing (Jack and Diane × Gelid Fish). The color of Love to Joe Marohl can be described as Pale ivory with a hint of purple, purple chevron eye that only shows faintly on the sepals above an extended, very green throat.. It is a Diploid. It has a Unusual FormCrispate-Cascade form. It has a bud count of 26. It blooms in (Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (6.5 in, (16 cm) ). It has 5 branches. It has a normal scape height of (40 in, (101 cm) ).
