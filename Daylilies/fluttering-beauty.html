Daylily description

The daylily Fluttering Beauty was hybridized by Joiner-J. in 1999. Fluttering Beauty was hybridized by crossing (sdlg × sdlg). The color of Fluttering Beauty can be described as ivory self with light green throat. It is a Diploid. It has a Unusual FormCrispateDouble 98% form. It has a bud count of 18. It blooms in (Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (9 in, (23 cm)). It has 3 branches. It has a normal scape height of (40 in, (102 cm)). It is Fragrant.
