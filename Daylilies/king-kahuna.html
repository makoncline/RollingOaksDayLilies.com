Daylily description

The daylily King Kahuna was hybridized by Crochet in 1994. King Kahuna was hybridized by crossing (Olin Frazier x Ellen Christine). The color of King Kahuna can be described as medium yellow self. It is a Diploid. It has a Double form. It blooms in (Early-Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (6.5 in, (17 cm)). It has a normal scape height of (22 in, (56 cm)).
