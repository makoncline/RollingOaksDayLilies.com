Daylily description

The daylily Marty DeBolt was hybridized by Breshears in 2007. Marty DeBolt was hybridized by crossing (sdlg x sdlg). The color of Marty DeBolt can be described as red orange self above green throat. It is a Diploid. It has a Double 100% form. It has a bud count of 35. It blooms in (Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (2.5 in, (6 cm)). It has 5 branches. It has a normal scape height of (24 in, (61 cm)).
