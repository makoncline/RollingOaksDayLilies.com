Daylily description

The daylily Wishy Washy was hybridized by Joiner-J. in 2013. Wishy Washy was hybridized by crossing (sdlg × sdlg). The color of Wishy Washy can be described as medium coral single first blooms, second blooms often are double, bright green to yellow throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a Double 50% form. It has a bud count of 30. It blooms in (Early,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (6.5 in, (17 cm)). It has 5 branches. It has a normal scape height of (31 in, (79 cm)).
