Daylily description

The daylily Let Loose was hybridized by Stamile in 2003. Let Loose was hybridized by crossing (His Highness × Tet. Garden Portrait). The color of Let Loose can be described as lemon yellow self above green throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a Unusual FormCrispate-Spatulate form. It has a bud count of 30. It blooms in (Extra Early,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (9.5 in, (24 cm) ). It has 4 branches. It has a normal scape height of (36 in, (91 cm) ). It is Very Fragrant.
