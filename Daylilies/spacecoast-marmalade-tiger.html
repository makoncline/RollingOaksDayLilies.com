Daylily description

The daylily Spacecoast Marmalade Tiger was hybridized by Kinnebrew-Gossard in 2016. Spacecoast Marmalade Tiger was hybridized by crossing ((Spacecoast Francis Busby × (My Sunny Valentine × Spacecoast Blood Diamond)) × Spacecoast Saltwater Taffy). The color of Spacecoast Marmalade Tiger can be described as orange with lighter watermark and edge, yellow green throat . It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 24. It blooms in (Early-Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (9 in, (23 cm)). It has 5 branches. It has a normal scape height of (34 in, (86 cm)). It is Fragrant.
