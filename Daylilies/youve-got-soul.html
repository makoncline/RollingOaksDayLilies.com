Daylily description

The daylily You've Got Soul was hybridized by Jaques in 2011. You've Got Soul was hybridized by crossing (Noonday Dreams × Suburban Chris Howell). The color of You've Got Soul can be described as pale lavender with large blue purple eye and edge, with a green throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 16. It blooms in (Early,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (5.25 in, (13 cm)). It has 3 branches. It has a normal scape height of (26 in, (66 cm)). It is Fragrant.
