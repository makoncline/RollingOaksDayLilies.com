    The daylily Truffle Smile was hybridized by Kirchhoff-D. in 2007. Truffle Smile was hybridized by crossing ((Big Peach Mama x (All About Eve x John Kirkland)) x Kay Day). The color of Truffle Smile can be described as coral pink rose with ruffled edge above yellow to green throat. Truffle Smile is a Tetraploid, has a Double 97% form, has a bud count of 30, blooms in (Early,Rebloom), it's flowers measure (5 in, (13 cm)), has 4 branches, has a normal scape height of (28 in, (71 cm)), and is Fragrant.
