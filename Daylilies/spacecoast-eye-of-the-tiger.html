Daylily description

The daylily Spacecoast Eye Of The Tiger was hybridized by Kinnebrew-Gossard in 2016. Spacecoast Eye Of The Tiger was hybridized by crossing (Spacecoast Flamingo Eyes × Viva Pinata). The color of Spacecoast Eye Of The Tiger can be described as dark salmon pink, puple eye. It is a Tetraploid. It has a Unusual FormCrispate form. It has a bud count of 30. It blooms in (Early-Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (10 in, (25 cm)). It has 5 branches. It has a normal scape height of (38 in, (97 cm)). It is Fragrant.
