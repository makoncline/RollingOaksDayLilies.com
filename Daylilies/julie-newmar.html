Daylily description

The daylily Julie Newmar was hybridized by Morss in 2000. Julie Newmar was hybridized by crossing ((sdlg x Fortune's Dearest) x Gerda Brooker). The color of Julie Newmar can be described as pastel peach with purple spider web washed eye and purple outlined gold edge above yellow to green throat. It is a Tetraploid. It has a bud count of 30. It blooms in (Early,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (7 in, (18 cm)). It has 4 branches. It has a normal scape height of (32 in, (81 cm)).
