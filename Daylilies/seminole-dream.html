Daylily description

The daylily Seminole Dream was hybridized by Kirchhoff-D. in 1990. Seminole Dream was hybridized by crossing ((Estelle Whitmire × Betty Woods) × Cabbage Flower). The color of Seminole Dream can be described as cool lemon yellow self with lime green throat. It is a Diploid. It has a Double form. It blooms in (Early-Midseason,Rebloom). It's flowers measure (4 in, (10 cm)). It has a normal scape height of (18 in, (46 cm)). It is Fragrant.
